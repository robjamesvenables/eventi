📋 Event Rundown
11 departments
Events Team
PIC
  • Events Manager leads all coordination on the day
  • Full team briefing at T-2 hours before doors open
  • Confirm all department heads are in position 30 mins before start
  • Client point of contact throughout — direct all client requests through Events Team
  • Walkthrough of venue completed by 10:00 day of event
  • Post-event debrief scheduled within 24 hours
Guest Relations
PIC
  • GRO team at main entrance from 30 mins before doors open
  • Registration table set up with wristbands, guest list and QR codes
  • Guests may bring family — allow entry with valid wristband
  • VIP guests — escort to designated area on arrival
  • Lanyards for organising committee — removed day-of for guests
Security & Health and Safety
PIC
  • Count all pax including adults & kids upon arrival
  • 2× additional security from Event Organizer on site
  • VIP Buggy on standby · 1 unit at entrance · 1 at perimeter
  • All vendor vehicles require load-in letter + company sticker ID
  • Health & Safety: First aid kit on site · Nearest hospital: BIMC Kuta · Emergency: +62 811 XXX · Evacuation point: Main car park
Audio Visual
PIC
Follow Technical Rundown — coordinate with production team

Included: Pioneer CDJ3000 ×2, V10 Mixer, Standard Lighting, Digital Mapping Projectors, Smoke Machine, CO₂ ×2, LED Screens ×4
Not Included (Client): Sound System, Generator ×4, Band Equipment, Mixer, Mics + Stands, Stage Risers, Artist Tents, Backstage Furniture
IT
PIC
  • WiFi network and password confirmed for event day
  • Live streaming setup confirmed — platform and login details shared with AV team
  • Screen content schedule (slides, videos, holding screens) confirmed T-24hrs
  • Tech support contact on standby throughout event
  • Backup internet connection arranged for mission-critical presentations
Kitchen
PIC
  • Head Chef briefed on full menu and service times
  • Dietary restrictions list confirmed and distributed to kitchen team
  • Live cooking station staffed from dinner service start
  • All food stalls set up and ready 30 mins before opening time
  • Halal requirements strictly observed — no cross-contamination
Bar
PIC
  • Bar Manager to confirm stock quantities before event
  • Bar opens non-alcoholic beverages first — alcoholic from scheduled time only
  • No service to visibly intoxicated guests
  • Underage guests — no alcoholic service under any circumstances
  • Leftover stock from first service area transferred to next bar
  • Cash and tab system confirmed with Finance team before doors open
Creative & Photography
PIC
  • All signage installed and checked by T-2 hours
  • Food tags, menus and printed materials distributed to relevant stations
  • Photography: photographer on site from doors open · Videographer from evening session
  • Drone permitted subject to venue approval — confirm timing with Events Team
  • No media access backstage without clearance from Events Team
Sales & Marketing
PIC
  • Social media coverage — coordinate with Events Team on posting schedule
  • All content requires client approval before going live
  • Press list confirmed with client PR team minimum 48hrs before event
  • Post-event recap content delivered within 48 hours of event close
Housekeeping
PIC
  • Full venue setup completed and inspected by T-3 hours
  • Towels prepared per event brief — swimmers and VIP areas
  • Bean bags, furniture and props arranged per layout plan
  • Continuous cleaning throughout event — team on rotation
  • Post-event breakdown begins immediately after close
Engineering
PIC
  • Barricade setup as per approved layout plan
  • All power connections checked and signed off before guest arrival
  • Generator backup confirmed and tested
  • On-call throughout event for any technical issues
Finance & Cashier
PIC
  • Payment status confirmed before event day — see Finance section
  • Cashier station set up and float confirmed
  • Any additional charges during event logged and approved by Events Manager
  • Final reconciliation completed within 24 hours post-event
